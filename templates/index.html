Why I Built This

As I started exploring undergraduate research at Purdue, I wanted a better way to organize professors by research interests and identify labs that aligned with my goals. I built this project to practice Python Flask, JavaScript filtering, frontend design, and research outreach preparation.

This tool helps students quickly search faculty profiles, compare research areas, and generate a starting point for personalized outreach emails.
